Full Body Workout

Balanced Strength for Every Muscle Group

Workout Plan

Warm up for 5-10 minutes. Perform 3-4 sets of each exercise with 60-90 seconds rest.

Squats

10-15 reps

Full lower body compound movement.

Deadlifts

8-12 reps

Great for posterior chain (back, glutes, hamstrings).

Bench Press

10-12 reps

Primary chest and triceps builder.

Bent-Over Rows

10-12 reps

Targets upper back and lats.

Overhead Press

10-12 reps

Shoulders and upper chest.
