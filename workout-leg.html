Workout Plan

Warm up for 5-10 minutes with light cardio and dynamic stretches. Perform 3-4 sets of each exercise with 60-90 seconds rest between sets.

Squats

10-12 reps

Focus on form: Keep your chest up, back straight, and go as low as comfortable.

Lunges

10 reps per leg

Alternate legs or do one side at a time for balance.

Leg Press

10-12 reps

Use controlled movement — avoid locking knees at the top.

Calf Raises

15-20 reps

Hold at the top for maximum contraction.
